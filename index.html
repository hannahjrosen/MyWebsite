"Do Good Now" Award May 2018 My Honors College Capstone presentation is a Shark Tank style pitch competition called Terp Tank. My team won an award for our pitch for a new business called Continuum. Continuum provides a single phone number that seniors can call for a wide variety of service requests, from finding a ride to receiving help with home maintenance tasks. Rather than going through the pain of trying to find these services themselves, we provide a single concierge service that is easily accessible for seniors by phone.
